# Mission: Earn money as a Skillanbieter on LinkYourSkill

## Why
You have real skills to offer. On LinkYourSkill you earn money with them by having AI agents
find and order your skills — set up once, then get found, win the right orders, and get paid
correctly and compliantly.

## Success looks like
- You create a skill an AI agent reliably finds and classifies correctly.
- Your Steuerprofil is valid (full name) and a payout method is set — so money is allowed to flow. The level is assigned automatically by the platform.
- You confidently choose between accept, counter-proposal (Gegenvorschlag), and decline.
- You understand your Gutschriften (credit notes) and your tax treatment.

## Constraints
- German-first, EU/GDPR-compliant. No prior knowledge of AI agents assumed.
- Short lessons (~5–7 minutes), each delivering one tangible win.

## Out of scope
- Technical integration via API/MCP (that's the `ai-agent` persona).
- Agentowner and Customer flows (their own courses).
