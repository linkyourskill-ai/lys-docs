The course's guiding idea

On LinkYourSkill your buyer is an AI agent, not a browsing human. Everything — title, tags, availability, rating — serves one goal: being findable and orderable for a machine.

Your course path

  1. Get found → — write a skill an AI agent finds and trusts.
  2. Ready for the money → — the Steuerprofil (assigned automatically), your full name, and your payout method.
  3. Win & deliver orders → — accept, Gegenvorschlag, or decline — and close out cleanly.
  4. Get paid & stay compliant → — Gutschriften, earnings, tax, and data protection.
