Lesson 04 · ~6 minutes · Knowledge & practice

What you'll be able to do

  • Understand when and how your Gutschrift is created.
  • Manage your consents and exercise your data rights.

From confirmed order to Gutschrift

As soon as the Agentowner confirms your completed order, the platform creates your Gutschrift (credit note) automatically — not before. You find your billing under Einnahmen (earnings): total, pending, and completed payments. Every Gutschrift can be viewed and downloaded as a PDF.

Net or gross — your Anbietertyp decides

How your Gutschrift is calculated depends on your Anbietertyp from lesson 02: with or without stated MwSt (VAT), net or gross. As a Kleinunternehmer it's usually issued without stated MwSt. Keep your Steuerprofil (tax profile) up to date and the billing stays correct.

Not tax advice

Net/gross and tax treatment here only explain how the platform bills — this is not tax advice. What applies to you for tax is something to settle, if in doubt, with your Steuerberater:in (tax advisor) or the Finanzamt (tax office).

Stay compliant: data protection & your data

  • Under Einstellungen → Datenschutz (Settings → Data protection) you control your consents: marketing emails, analytics, and AI profiling — switchable any time.
  • Every change is logged, so it stays traceable what you allowed and when.
  • You can have your account deleted on request (GDPR Art. 17) — via Einstellungen → Gefahrenzone (Danger Zone).

Core idea

You get paid only once the Agentowner confirms — then the Gutschrift is created automatically. Your data and consents stay in your own hands at all times.

Knowledge check

When does the platform generate your Gutschrift?

Only the Agentowner's confirmation triggers the Gutschrift — your own "mark as completed" isn't enough, and publishing your skill has nothing to do with billing. The confirmation is the trigger.

Primary source

See it in the app: Einnahmen for your Gutschriften, and Einstellungen → Datenschutz for your consents.
