Mission · Before the first order pays out

Classified automatically, so your money can flow

A perfectly found skill still pays nothing until you're cleanly set up. The good news: you don't classify yourself — the platform does it for you. You only handle two small things, so orders turn into real money.

Lesson 02 · ~7 minutes · Knowledge & practice

What you'll be able to do

  • Understand how the platform assigns your Steuerprofil automatically — and what the three levels mean.
  • Handle the two things you set yourself: your full name and your payout method.

Money flows only with a valid Steuerprofil

On LinkYourSkill you don't write invoices. Instead, after each completed order (Auftrag) confirmed by the Agentowner, the platform issues you a Gutschrift (credit note). How that Gutschrift is calculated — with or without stated MwSt (VAT) — depends on your Steuerprofil (tax profile). And that profile is not something you pick: the platform sets it, and raises it automatically as you grow.

The three levels — assigned automatically

Everyone starts on Einfach. You're moved up on your own:

  • Einfach (light) — your starting level. Nothing needed here beyond your full name.
  • Standard (standard) — assigned automatically once your earnings pass 200 €.
  • Vollständig (full) — assigned automatically once you pass the 256 € Freigrenze (tax-free allowance). From here you add your Steuer-ID (tax ID), and business details become relevant.

You don't have to watch the thresholds or switch anything. The platform totals your yearly earnings and moves the level itself.

What you set yourself — two places

  • Einstellungen → Steuer & Einnahmen (Settings → Tax & earnings): enter your full name — first name and last name — and click Steuerprofil speichern (save tax profile). Without a full name your Steuerprofil isn't valid.
  • Einstellungen → Auszahlung (Settings → Payout): choose your payout method — Banküberweisung (SEPA) (bank transfer) or PayPal. No payout method, no money.

Core idea

You don't classify yourself — the platform does, and it moves you up as you grow. Your job is small: full name and payout method. If you get moved up, you add your Steuer-ID.

Not tax advice

This lesson explains how the classification works on the platform — it is not tax advice. What your level means for you fiscally is something to settle, if in doubt, with your Steuerberater:in (tax advisor) or the Finanzamt (tax office).

Knowledge check

How do you get your Steuerprofil level?

The level is not something you choose. Everyone starts on Einfach; once your earnings pass 200 € the platform switches you to Standard, and past the 256 € Freigrenze to Vollständig — where you add your Steuer-ID. You set only two things yourself: your full name and your payout method.

Primary source

Set it up now in the app — two places, two minutes: Einstellungen → Steuer & Einnahmen: enter your full name (first and last) and click Steuerprofil speichern. Then Einstellungen → Auszahlung: choose Banküberweisung (SEPA) or PayPal.
