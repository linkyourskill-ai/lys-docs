Money flows only after classification

On LinkYourSkill you don't write invoices. Instead, after each completed order (Auftrag) confirmed by the Agentowner, the platform issues you a Gutschrift (credit note). How that Gutschrift is calculated — with or without stated MwSt (VAT) — depends on your Anbietertyp (provider type). So you classify yourself once before money can flow.

The four provider types

During setup you pick one option under Anbietertyp:

  • Privatperson — you offer as a private individual.
  • Kleinunternehmer (§19 UStG) — small business; your Gutschrift is usually issued without stated MwSt.
  • Gewerbetreibender — registered trader; business details (Firmierung, USt-IdNr, Handelsregisternr.) are required.
  • Freiberufler — freelancer; also with business details.

What your type triggers

  • Gutschrift with or without MwSt: your type decides net/gross. Kleinunternehmer → without stated MwSt.
  • Required before the first order: as a Gewerbetreibender or Freiberufler you must add your business details (e.g. USt-IdNr) before you can accept your first order.
  • Self-employment declaration: you confirm the Selbständigkeitserklärung once during setup.
  • Kleinunternehmer threshold: the platform totals your yearly earnings so you can see when you're approaching the threshold.

Set up once: Steuerprofil & bank details

You finish the rest under Einstellungen → Steuerprofil (Settings → Tax profile): provider type, business details (USt-IdNr, Handelsregisternr.) and — crucially — your bank details for payouts. Without bank details on file, there's no payout. These settings also drive how your Gutschriften are calculated.

Core idea

Your classification isn't a checkbox to click away — it decides whether and how you get paid. Set it honestly once, and the money flows correctly.

Not tax advice

This lesson explains how classification works on the platform — it is not tax advice. Which type applies to you is something to settle, if in doubt, with your Steuerberater:in (tax advisor) or the Finanzamt (tax office).

Knowledge check

What must a Gewerbetreibender complete before their first order?

For Gewerbetreibender and Freiberufler, the business details — such as the USt-IdNr — are required before the first order can be accepted. Publishing skills and collecting ratings belong to getting found, not to the payout prerequisites.
