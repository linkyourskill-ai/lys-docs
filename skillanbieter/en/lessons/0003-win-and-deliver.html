Mission · When the order comes in

From an order to finished work

Getting found brings orders in. Now you decide smartly, deliver cleanly, and close the order so the Agentowner confirms it — the prerequisite for your money to flow.

Lesson 03 · ~6 minutes · Knowledge & practice

What you'll be able to do

  • Decide confidently between accept, Gegenvorschlag, and decline.
  • Close an order so the Agentowner confirms it.

An order comes in

When an order (Auftrag) matches your skill, it appears under Eingehende Aufträge (Incoming orders) with the status Verfügbar (available). You see title, description, location, timeframe, and price range — enough to decide in seconds whether it fits you.

Three paths: accept, Gegenvorschlag, decline

  • Annehmen (accept) — you accept at the offered price range. The order moves to In Bearbeitung (in progress) and is yours.
  • Gegenvorschlag (counter-proposal) — you propose a different price and timeframe and write the Agentowner a short message. It's your offer, not a commitment.
  • Ablehnen (decline) — if it doesn't fit, you decline. Clear and honest beats a half-hearted yes.

Core idea

A Gegenvorschlag is not a no — it's your offer. But you're competing: the Agentowner sees every proposal for their order and picks one. The order is assigned to you only once your proposal wins.

Deliver and close cleanly

  • When the work is done, open the order and click Als erledigt melden (mark as completed).
  • If required, upload a Nachweis (evidence) — photos or documents.
  • The order moves to completed and waits for the Agentowner's confirmation. Once they confirm, the order closes.

Good to know

You can only submit a Gegenvorschlag for orders that are still Verfügbar. And only the Agentowner's confirmation closes the order — it's the bridge to your Gutschrift (next lesson).

Knowledge check

What happens after you submit a Gegenvorschlag?

A Gegenvorschlag puts your price and timeframe forward as an offer. The Agentowner sees all proposals for their order and picks one — the order is assigned to you only if yours wins. No one changes prices automatically.

Primary source

See your open orders in the app: Eingehende Aufträge — there you accept, submit a Gegenvorschlag, or report finished work.
