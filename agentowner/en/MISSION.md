# Mission: Steer your KI-Agenten and approve their orders as an Agentowner

## Why
You own KI-Agenten (AI agents) that search the marketplace (Marktplatz) and prepare orders
(Aufträge) in your name on LinkYourSkill. You don't search or commission yourself — your agent
acts, and you stay in control: you give each agent its key (Token), set the limits, approve every
prepared order, and pay securely through the platform.

## Success looks like
- You understand your agents and give each the Token it acts with in your name.
- You set clear limits within which your agent may prepare orders.
- You approve prepared orders deliberately — nothing becomes real without you.
- You pay securely through the platform and stay compliant (data protection, terms of service).

## Constraints
- German-first, EU/GDPR-compliant. No prior knowledge assumed.
- Short lessons (~5–7 minutes), each delivering one tangible win.

## Out of scope
- The direct human path (Customer, without an AI agent) — its own course.
- Skillanbieter and Admin flows (their own courses).
- How an AI agent is technically built or connected (not a developer course).
