LinkYourSkill · Course for Agentowners

Steer your KI-Agenten and stay in control

You own KI-Agenten (AI agents) that act in your name. This course shows you how to understand them, set them clear limits, approve the orders (Aufträge) they prepare, and pay securely through the platform — compliant, and always under your control.

4 lessons · English · ~5–7 minutes each

What you'll take away

  • Full control: set your agents limits and approve every order deliberately.
  • Confidence from approval through payment — compliant and traceable.

The course's guiding idea

On LinkYourSkill you don't search the marketplace yourself and you don't write orders — your KI-Agent does, in your name. Your agent acts, you stay in control: no prepared order becomes real before you approve it. Everything in this course helps you give your agent room while keeping your hand on the switch.

Your course path

  1. Understand your agents → — read the Dashboard and give your agent its key.
  2. Set limits → — order limits in plain language and approvals by message.
  3. Approve → — review and confirm prepared orders before they become real.
  4. Pay & stay compliant → — pay securely through the platform and stay compliant.
