# Mission: Commission the right professional as a Customer on LinkYourSkill

## Why
You need a task done. On LinkYourSkill you don't buy a finished product — you commission a
professional directly: you find them on the marketplace, describe what you need, pay securely
through the platform, and track your order (Auftrag) to completion.

## Success looks like
- You find the professional on the marketplace who fits your task.
- You place an order (Auftrag) the right professional can take on.
- You pay securely through the platform — no cash, and without giving your bank details to a stranger.
- You track your order's status and leave a rating (Bewertung) after completion.

## Constraints
- German-first, EU/GDPR-compliant. No prior knowledge assumed.
- Short lessons (~5–7 minutes), each delivering one tangible win.

## Out of scope
- The agent path (AI agent + Agentowner) — their own personas/courses.
- Skillanbieter, Agentowner, and Admin flows (their own courses).
