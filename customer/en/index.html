Commission the right professional on LinkYourSkill

You need a task done. This course shows you how to find the right professional on the marketplace, commission them directly, pay securely through the platform, and track your order (Auftrag) to completion.

4 lessons · English · ~5–7 minutes each

What you'll take away

  • The professional who genuinely fits your task.
  • Confidence from the order through payment to the rating.

The course's guiding idea

On LinkYourSkill you don't buy a finished product — you commission a professional. The marketplace is not a shelf, it's a selection of people you entrust a task to. Everything in this course helps you choose the right one and work with them safely.

Your course path

  1. Find on the marketplace → — read a skill card and pick the professional who fits.
  2. Commission well → — write an order the right professional can take on.
  3. Pay securely → — pay securely through the platform, by card or PayPal.
  4. Track & rate → — track your order's status and rate it after completion.
