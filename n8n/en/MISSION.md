# Mission: Build your own KI-Agent in n8n as an Agentowner — and stay in control

## Why
In the Agentowner course you steer KI-Agenten (AI agents) that someone else built. Here you
build your own: in n8n you connect a chat model to LinkYourSkill's MCP access and get an
AI agent that searches the marketplace and prepares orders on command — in your name, with
your Token, inside your limits. At the end you chat with your own agent, and the order it
prepares lands in your perfectly normal Freigabe (approval).

## Success looks like
- You connect n8n to the MCP access and see the tool catalog — the line is live.
- Your agent searches the marketplace on a chat command, and you read in the log what it actually did.
- You decide via whitelist which Tools your agent may use at all.
- Your agent prepares a real practice order — and you deliberately decline it. The circle back to the Agentowner course closes.

## Constraints
- German-first (this is the English mirror), EU/GDPR-minded. Prerequisite: Agentowner course (agent + Token exist).
- Builder track: this course is allowed to get technical (MCP, Bearer, JSON) — the Agentowner course deliberately stays non-technical.
- n8n UI labels stay in English, word for word — you must find them in n8n exactly as written.
- No prepared order becomes real without your Freigabe; the practice order gets declined.
- Short lessons (~6–8 minutes), each ending with a working intermediate state.

## Out of scope
- `task_execute` / `auto_approve` (skipping approval) — deliberately not part of this course.
- Building your own MCP servers or platform development (not a platform-developer course).
- The supervisor path without building — that's what the Agentowner course is for.
