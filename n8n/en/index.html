Your agent in n8n — you build it yourself

In the Agentowner course you learned to steer KI-Agenten (AI agents). In this course you build your own: in n8n you connect a chat model to LinkYourSkill's MCP access — and at the end you chat with an agent that searches the marketplace and prepares orders for you. Approving them is still your job.

4 lessons · English · ~6–8 minutes each

What you'll take away

  • A working KI-Agent in n8n — connected with your Token, restricted to the Tools you allow.
  • The look behind the scenes: you read in the log what your agent really does — and stay in control.

The course's through-line

"Your agent acts — you stay in control" holds here unchanged. What's new is your role: you no longer just steer, you build. Every lesson ends with a working intermediate state, and no order your agent prepares becomes real before you approve it.

Your course path

  1. Connect → — n8n meets the MCP access: a first connection without any AI, using your Token.
  2. Your agent searches → — attach a chat model, restrict the Tools, let it search the marketplace.
  3. Your agent orders → — a real practice order is created and lands in your Freigabe (approval).
